About

Hi, I'm Daniel, a curious programmer born in Saudi Arabia and raised in the Philippines. After earning a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, I began pursuing my passion for Web Development. Sculpting a website from scratch has been an art form that I have come to appreciate. To deliver high-quality code and content, I make a conscious effort to put my audiences first.

I love learning and am genuinely grateful for the internet and its limitless number of resources. On my site, you'll find a little more information about me and a short list of informational websites that I use often.
